PhD Candidate in Economics

Lennart Goldemann

University of Konstanz

I am a PhD candidate in economics at the University of Konstanz under the supervision of Prof. Sebastian Findeisen and Prof. Guido Schwerdt.

My research is in applied microeconomics, with interests in labor economics, the economics of education, and inequality.

I study how family background shapes labor-market careers, and more broadly how inequalities emerge across educational, institutional, and labor-market settings.
